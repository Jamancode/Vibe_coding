FAQ

How do I enroll in a course?

Click on any course and then click "Enroll". You'll be guided through the enrollment process step by step.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, bank transfers, and SEPA direct debit.

Can I get a refund if I cancel my enrollment?

Yes, you can cancel courses up to 14 days before the course start date free of charge. After that, cancellation fees may apply.

How do I access course materials?

Once enrolled, you'll receive login credentials to access our learning platform with all course materials, videos, and assignments.
